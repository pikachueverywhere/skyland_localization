Bonemeal a large grass area and use a sickle to harvest the tall grass to obtain seeds and fruit saplings. Food is an important consideration for your survival on this map.  Mine Factory Reloaded planters and harvesters can be used to create farms for the Harvestcraft seeds. And the Fruit Picker can be used to harvest fully grown fruit from the saplings. Sludge from the harvesters can be piped into a Sludge Boiler to produce any dirt or sand type block in the game at random. This is an excellent source of extra dirt for building farms.
