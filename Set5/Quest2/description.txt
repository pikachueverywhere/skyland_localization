To begin your journey into the wonderful world of magic and sorcery you will require special tools. For Blood Magic you will be harnessing the power of your life essence to cast spells and create powerful artifacts. Be careful you don't die. Thaumcraft starts with a bookshelf and a wand. Right click your wand on a bookshelf to create a Thauminomicon. All of the information needed for progressing through Thaumcraft is contained inside that book.
