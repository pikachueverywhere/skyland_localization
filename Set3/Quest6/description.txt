To get started on near infinite power your first step is to create a reactor. You will need casings, fuel rods and controllers. The most basic reactor is a 3x3x3 cube with a yellorium fuel rod in the center. The reactor controller, powertap and access port all go in the second level in the middle of one of the sides. The top tier has a Reactor control rod in the center sorrounded by reactors casings. Right click the access port to insert fuel then right click the controller and press activate to start the process. This reactor can store up to 10million RF at a time.
